Frequently Asked Questions

How is JSC's hackathon different this year?

In previous years, JSC's Hackathon has been held solely for the NASA JSC workforce, spanning all directorates, programs, and disciplines. This year, we will be inviting the Houston educational community to participate and network with JSC participants. Teams of all skill levels will work together with the ultimate goal of collaborating, exploring, and building solutions to current complex agency challenges on the Artemis and Moon to Mars mission.

We are partnering with The Ion for the first time to host JSC Hack 2021. Located at the heart of Downtown Houston, this 266,000-square foot structure is designed to bring Houston's entrepreneurial, corporate, and academic communities together into collaborative spaces to solve some of the world's greatest problems.

Who can attend?

Participants of all ages may attend. Attendees under 18 must confirm upon registration that they have consent from a parent or guardian. If applications surpass our capacity, college graduate/undergraduate students will be given first priority. All Johnson Space Center employees are guaranteed acceptance.

Regardless of your experience, education, or background, as long as you are excited about learning, building, and having fun, weâ€™d love for you to attend.

Absolutely zero! We want you here because you have a passion for solving problems, and you don't have to be a programmer. Weâ€™ll have workshops and mentors for everyone of all levels of expertise to learn something new.

This event is completely free for everyone. Food and swag are included.

I cannot attend in-person. Can I participate remotely?

Teams submitting projects must have at least one team member present to demo on Sunday 1/30 from 10am-12pm, so while we cannot support all-virtual teams (including teams of one participant), you may join a partially in-person team to submit a project eligible for judging.

While we cannot guarantee that virtual participants will be able to participate in all hackathon activities, we will do our best to include virtual participants in any events that are streaming-capable via Slack.
